1.Request and Response:-
----------------------------------

3.service provider or server consumer :-
-------------------------------------------------

Application A ------------------ webservice(to-do's aplication)


--Application A wants to consume a service from the webservice which is present in here.
-- the webservice here is called a service provider-Service provider is the one which hosts the webservice.
--service provider is the guy who's is providing the service is called service provider.

4.service consumer or client
-------------------------------------
the service consumer is the one which is consuming the webservice.
--Application A is Consumer of the webservice
-- the service consumer is the guy who is using the service.

5.service definition:- 
---------------------------
the service definition is the contract between the service provider and the service consumer.
The service definition defines what is the format of the request and response, XML or JSON?
What is the structure of the request?
What is the structure of the response?
And where is the service available?
That's the endpoint.Endpoint defines what URL the service is exposed at.
How can this service consumer call this service which is exposed by the service provider?


6.what is transport:-
---------------------------

Transport defines how a service is called
is service exposed over internet?---so i'll be given a URL, and I'll call it. 
or 
is the service exposed over a queue? ---- two popular format HTTP and MQ
Http is basically all the web and just like you type a URL in the browser,

The other format is MQ.It's to use communication over a queue.The service requester would place a message in the queue.The service provider would be listening on the queue.And as soon as there's a request on the queue,it would take the request, do the processing of it,create the response, and put it back in the queue,and the service requester would get the response from the queue.The transport which is used is MQ.

-It does not necessarily be WebSphere MQ.It can be any of the other MQs as well,but the fact is the communication happens over queue.
which is exposed over the transport MQ.


--Transport indicates whether the service is exposed over a queue or whether it's exposed over the web, that's the internet.
